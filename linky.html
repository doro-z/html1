LEGO is AWESOME This website talks about everything LEGO, such as what it is, the history behind the brick, design, manufacturing, and themes. LEGO actually started off as wooden toys. With the current molded plastic bricks, you can build anything your creative imagainaion allows. Some themes in LEGO are Star Wars, Architecture, Marvel, DC, Ninjago, Creator, Minecraft, Super Mario, and more. LEGO recently promised to go green by 2025. Now, all their plant bricks are made from plant-based and recycled materials like sugarcane and recycled plastic bottles. LEGO is always striving to improve play.
